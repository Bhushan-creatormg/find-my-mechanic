Auto Tips & DIY Help

How to Check Engine Oil?

Park on level ground, let engine cool, pull out dipstick, wipe clean, insert again, then check oil level.

Battery Dead? Try This

Use jumper cables from another car, connect positive to positive and negative to engine block, then start the car.
